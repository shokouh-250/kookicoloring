# Kooki Coloring - Phase 1 Roadmap

## Objective

Simplify the website, reduce visual clutter, improve product discovery, and create a cleaner user journey.

This phase focuses only on:

1. Homepage Simplification
2. Dedicated Shop Page
3. Search Improvements

Do not implement reviews, ratings, multilingual support, AI chat, wishlist, or Instagram gallery in this phase.

## Success Criteria

The homepage should feel cozy, clean, premium, uncluttered, and easy to scan.

Visitors should immediately understand:

- What Kooki is
- Where to browse books
- Where to get free coloring pages
- How to follow Kooki on social media

## Homepage Simplification

Remove duplicate product sections. A product should appear only once on the homepage.

Do not create separate homepage sections for Best Sellers, Physical Books, or Digital Downloads.

New homepage order:

1. Hero Section
2. Featured Books
3. Free Coloring Pages
4. Social Community Section
5. Newsletter Section
6. Footer

## Dedicated Shop Page

Create `/shop`.

The homepage should inspire, introduce, and guide. The shop page should contain the full catalog, filtering, sorting, and product discovery.

Filters:

- All Products
- Coloring Books
- Digital Downloads
- Free Downloads

Sorting:

- Newest
- Name A-Z
- Name Z-A
- Price Low-High
- Price High-Low

Prepare architecture for future sorting by rating and popularity, but do not implement those features in Phase 1.

## Search Improvements

Search should match product title, partial title, keywords, tags, collections, and product descriptions.

Search must be case insensitive, partial-match friendly, fast, and responsive.

Search results should display matching products only and show a clear empty state when no results exist.

## Out of Scope for Phase 1

Do not implement:

- Ratings
- Reviews
- Testimonials
- Instagram gallery automation
- AI support chat
- Wishlist/favorites
- User accounts
- Multilingual support
- Blog section

## Final Design Principles

Always prioritize simplicity over features, quality over quantity, one product shown once, homepage as discovery, shop page as catalog, Amazon/Etsy as purchase destinations, social media as a community hub, the cozy Kooki aesthetic, low visual clutter, and effortless navigation.
